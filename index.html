Our Story

Crafting Tomorrow's Classics

At LUXE, we believe a bag is more than an accessory—it's an expression of who you are. Founded in Milan in 1992, we've spent decades perfecting the art of creating pieces that honor traditional leather craftsmanship while embracing contemporary design.

Each piece in our collection is handcrafted by skilled artisans using ethically sourced premium leathers. From the initial sketch to the final stitch, we obsess over every detail to ensure your bag stands the test of time—both in style and durability.
